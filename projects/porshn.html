What is porshn?

Ever grown tired of chasing your friends for money?

Ever wished for one place to log all your split bills and expenses?


porshn is a simple app that splits bills and expenses across a group of users. By centralising users into private groups, all users can be notified of new or incoming bills, equally assign upcoming or paid bills and delegate/rotate chores, and even extend to using chores as payment!

Why It Matters

As a university student, I can relate to the situation we face when a bill is on the table.
Seeing as we are busy studying, we are often caught being unable to pay our fair share.
porshn has been conceived to ensure that this doesn't have to be the case, with split bills being easily sorted whether paid or incoming, in a social/group setting.

Tech Stack

Backend: TypeScript, NestJS, Prisma, PostgreSQL
Mobile: React Native (Expo)
